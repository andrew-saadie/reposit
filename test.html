Food

My Favorite Recipe: Spaghetti Carbonara

Spaghetti Carbonara is one of my favorite dishes to make. It's a classic Italian pasta dish that's creamy, savory, and delicious. To make it, you'll need spaghetti, bacon, eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper. Cook the spaghetti and bacon, then mix the eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper in a bowl. Add the spaghetti and bacon to the bowl and mix well. Serve hot and enjoy!
